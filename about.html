I am a Graphic Designer currently based in Lisbon, Portugal. My work is heavily influenced by my time living in South Africa, Portugal and Brazil (with some time spent living in Denmark), and picking up a lot of different references. I bring an obscure, fun, predominantly non-western, mysterious and queer aspect to my work. Visually influenced by the decolonised and non-western world of art, mysticism, fashion, photography, and internet trash culture; I like to create experimental, abstract, loud and cunty* visuals in the areas of poster design, editorial design, motion design, branding and identity.
