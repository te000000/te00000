Syfy
2024
This academic work is a rebranding and motion design done for the television channel SYFY. In these four videos for the identity, continuity, programme bumper and instagram video, I created videos that felt like they were a fever-dream, and like they were retro-futuristic so as to give them this weird, quirky humour some sci-fi films have. These visuals were also an ode to the late 90s visuals this channel adopted as an identity within the first years of its launch.
Academic Project, Motion Design, Branding & Identity
