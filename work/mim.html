MiM
2024
This piece of work is a conjunction of a poster and three instagram posts I designed for a party collective called MiM that occurs in Johannesburg, South Africa. This party is a celebration of all things house and dance music, and so my intentions with this package was to make something fun, and quirky, energetic, and c*nty!
