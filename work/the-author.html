The Author
2025
In a world that is increasingly being influenced by AI and mechanisms, this collaborative academic project done with Inês Pereira and Rodrigo Brandini, we questioned the role and influence of AI in the creative industry and the work that we do as graphic desginers. After all, who actually is the author and owner of a piece of artistic work? This project consists of three research books, a manifesto, posters, and merchandising.
Collaborative Project, Academic Project, Authorial Project, Editorial Design
